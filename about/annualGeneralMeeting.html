2025 Annual General Meeting

Our next Annual General Meeting will be held on June 15, 2025, at 7:00 PM. All GCBA members are encouraged to attend this important event where we'll review the past season, discuss plans for the upcoming year, and elect new board members.

Meeting Details:

  • Date: June 15, 2025
  • Time: 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM
  • Location: Gloucester Community Center, Orleans
  • Hybrid format: In-person and virtual attendance options

Agenda:

  1. Welcome and introductions
  2. Approval of previous AGM minutes
  3. President's report
  4. Treasurer's report and financial statements
  5. Program directors' reports
  6. Election of board members
  7. Strategic planning discussion
  8. Open forum for member questions and feedback
  9. Adjournment

All members in good standing are entitled to vote at the AGM. If you cannot attend in person, you will have the option to participate virtually. Registration details will be emailed to all members two weeks prior to the meeting.

Board Positions Up for Election

  • Vice President

    Two-year term, responsible for supporting the president and overseeing program development.

  • Treasurer

    Two-year term, responsible for financial management and reporting.

  • House League Director

    Two-year term, responsible for the recreational basketball program.

  • Referee Development Director

    Two-year term, responsible for referee recruitment, training, and scheduling.
