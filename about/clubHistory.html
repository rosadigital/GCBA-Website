Our History

Since 1981, GCBA has been building community through basketball in Gloucester and the greater Ottawa area.

The GCBA Story

1981

Our Foundation

GCBA was established as a registered non-profit organization dedicated to fostering a love for basketball in the City of Ottawa. Founded on the principle of creating a supportive, community-driven environment, we began providing youth ages 5 to 19 with opportunities to learn, grow, and compete.

Program Growth

Expanding Our Reach

As we grew, GCBA developed our cornerstone programs: House League focusing on fundamental skill development, and Competitive Program designed for players looking to advance team play and strategy. These programs continue to serve as the foundation of our organization, with Fall/Winter seasons running from September through March.

Tournament Legacy

Signature Events

GCBA established itself as a tournament host, proudly organizing three major tournaments each year that draw 180+ teams from across Ontario, Quebec, and New York State. These include the Mike O'Connor Memorial Tournament (February/March), Mike Stys Invitational (February/March), and serving as an Ontario Basketball Championships Host Club (April/May).

Youth Development

Program Innovation

Our commitment to youth development led to the creation of specialized programs like our Jr. NBA Program, Spring League, Summer 3-on-3 Leagues, and Junior Referee Development Program. These initiatives provide diverse opportunities for young athletes to engage with basketball year-round while developing both skills and character.

Today

Eastern Ontario's Premier Club

GCBA has grown to become the largest and longest-running volunteer basketball club in Eastern Ontario. As an affiliated member of Ontario Basketball and the Eastern Ontario Basketball Committee, we now serve over 1,500+ annual participants across our House League and Competitive programs, with 600-1,000+ players every season and 80+ games every weekend during our Fall/Winter season.

Volunteer Members

Volunteer-Driven Excellence

As a proudly volunteer-run organization, GCBA's success is built on the dedication of over 200+ community members who contribute to the success of our programs each year. We operate under the governance of an executive committee consisting of elected officers and an appointed Executive Director, ensuring smooth club operations in accordance with our Constitution.

Our impact extends beyond just playing basketball. We've created a community where young athletes ages 5 to 19 can learn, grow, and compete in a supportive environment. Through our house league, competitive programs, and special initiatives, we continue to serve players of all abilities across Orleans and Ottawa East.
