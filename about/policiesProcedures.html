Volunteer Screening & Certification

GCBA is committed to providing a safe environment for all participants. All coaches, assistant coaches, and volunteers who work directly with children are required to complete the following:

  • Police Record Check (PRC) with Vulnerable Sector Screening
  • Completion of Respect in Sport for Activity Leaders certification
  • Signed Code of Conduct agreement
  • Completion of NCCP FUNdamentals or equivalent coaching certification (for head coaches)
  • Concussion awareness training
