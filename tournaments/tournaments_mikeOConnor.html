Mike O'Connor Memorial Girls' Tournament

A prestigious girls' basketball tournament honoring the legacy of Mike O'Connor and celebrating female athletes.

Tournament Overview

The Mike O'Connor Memorial Girls' Tournament is GCBA's premier event for female basketball players, bringing together competitive teams from across the region for a weekend of high-level competition, sportsmanship, and community.

Mike O'Connor was a dedicated coach and advocate for girls' basketball, whose passion and commitment to developing female athletes left an indelible mark on our community. This tournament honors his legacy by providing opportunities for girls to showcase their talents and compete at the highest level.

Age Divisions – U12, U14, U16, and U19 girls

3-Game Guarantee – Every team plays at least three games

Championship Sunday – Exciting playoff format

Quality Facilities – Games at premier venues across the city
