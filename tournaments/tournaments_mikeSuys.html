Mike Suys Boys' Invitational

A prestigious tournament honoring the legacy of Michel "Mike" Suys, founder of the Gloucester Basketball Association.

Tournament Overview

The Mike Suys Boys' Invitational Tournament (MSBI)—formerly known as the Gloucester Wolverines Boys Invitational—enters its second year under its new name, honoring the late Michel "Mike" Suys.

Mike Suys founded the Gloucester Basketball Association (GBA) in 1981, which later evolved into the GCBA. He played a pivotal role in launching the first GBA invitational tournament in 1985.

This prestigious annual tournament is officially sanctioned by the Ontario Basketball Association (OBA) and contributes to the GCBA Benevolent Fund.
