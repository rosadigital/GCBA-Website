Who We Are

The Gloucester-Cumberland Basketball Association (GCBA) is a non-profit, community-based basketball organization operating in the Orleans area since 1981.

It provides opportunities for children aged 5-19 to participate in basketball.

Over 200 community volunteers contribute to the success of the association.
