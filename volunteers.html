Volunteer with GCBA

Be a part of something special. GCBA relies on dedicated volunteers to make our programs successful for hundreds of young athletes.

Why Volunteer?

The Gloucester Cumberland Basketball Association is a volunteer-driven organization. Every season, we rely on more than 200 dedicated volunteers to help run our programs, coach our teams, organize our events, and support our players. Without volunteers, we simply couldn't offer the quality basketball experience that has become our hallmark.

Volunteering with GCBA is a rewarding way to give back to your community while helping young athletes develop both on and off the court.

Becoming a Volunteer

Requirements

  • Background Check: All volunteers working directly with youth must complete a Police Record Check. GCBA provides the necessary documentation for obtaining this check.
  • Code of Conduct: All volunteers must agree to and sign the GCBA Volunteer Code of Conduct.
  • Training (Role-specific): Different volunteer positions may require specific training. For example, coaches need NCCP certification.

Process

  1. Submit an Application

    Complete our volunteer application form indicating your areas of interest and availability.

  2. Interview/Orientation

    Depending on the role, you may have a brief discussion with a coordinator about expectations and responsibilities.

  3. Background Check

    Complete the necessary screening process for your role.

  4. Training

    Attend any required training sessions for your specific role.

  5. Placement

    You'll be matched with a team, program, or event based on needs and your preferences.
