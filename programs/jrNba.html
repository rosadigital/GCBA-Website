Jr. NBA Program

An exciting introductory basketball program designed for young athletes ages 5-7, following the official NBA curriculum.

Program Overview

The Jr. NBA program introduces young players to basketball in a fun, supportive environment. Using the official NBA curriculum, we focus on developing fundamental skills while fostering a love for the game.

NBA-Designed Curriculum – Learn from proven teaching methods

Age-Appropriate Activities – Fun drills and games for young players

Fundamental Skills – Focus on basic movement and basketball skills

Positive Environment – Build confidence and make friends

Program Details

Schedule & Location

  • Saturday Mornings (1 hour sessions)
  • Local East End Gyms
  • 12-Week Program

What to Bring

  • Athletic Clothing & Running Shoes
  • Water Bottle
  • Positive Attitude!

Skills Development Focus

Basic Movement

  • Running & Stopping
  • Balance & Coordination
  • Jumping & Landing

Ball Handling

  • Dribbling Basics
  • Ball Control
  • Hand-Eye Coordination

Team Play

  • Passing & Catching
  • Sharing the Ball
  • Following Instructions
