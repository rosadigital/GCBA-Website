Competitive Program

Elite-level basketball for dedicated players looking to advance their skills and compete at higher levels across the province.

Program Overview

The GCBA Wolverines Competitive Program represents our elite-level basketball offering, providing advanced training and high-level competition for dedicated players. Our teams compete in the Ontario Basketball Association (OBA) leagues and tournaments throughout the province.

This challenging program focuses on developing well-rounded players through intensive skill training, strategic team play, and competitive games against top-tier opponents. Players receive coaching from experienced, NCCP-certified staff committed to maximizing athletes' potential.

Program Highlights

  • Ontario Basketball Association (OBA) league participation
  • Multiple practices per week led by certified coaches
  • Tournament participation across Ontario
  • Advanced skill development through specialized training

Program Details

Coaching & Development

All GCBA Wolverines teams are led by NCCP certified coaches with extensive basketball knowledge and coaching experience. Our coaching philosophy emphasizes skill development, tactical team play, and character building.

Development Focus Areas

Advanced individual skills
Tactical team play
Athletic development
Basketball IQ
Mental toughness
Competitive mindset

Season Structure

Training

  • 2-3 team practices per week
  • Position-specific skills sessions
  • Strength & conditioning programs

Competition

  • 6-8 tournaments per season
  • Ontario Basketball regular season games
  • Ontario Cup Championships participation

Program Fees

Competitive program fees range from $1,000-$1,400 per season, depending on the age division. Fees cover:

  • Coaching and training fees
  • Ontario Basketball registration
  • Insurance coverage
  • Home tournament entry fees
  • Uniform package
  • Administrative costs

Note: Additional fees for away tournaments and Ontario Cup Championships will be collected separately.
