Coaches' Corner

Essential resources and support to help you develop successful, confident basketball players.

Welcome, Coaches!

Your role as a coach is vital to our mission of developing not just basketball players, but confident young people who learn valuable life skills through sport. The GCBA Coaches' Corner provides tools, training, and support to help you succeed in this important work.

Whether you're a first-time volunteer or an experienced coach, these resources will help you create positive, productive practices and games while developing your players' skills and love for basketball.

Ready-to-Use Practice Plans – Age-appropriate activities to keep your team engaged and improving

NCCP Certification Support – Guidance to help you meet requirements and expand your coaching knowledge

One-on-One Mentorship – Connect with experienced coaches for personalized guidance

Coach Quick Details

  • Upcoming Sessions: Pre-season workshops in September
  • Required Training: NCCP appropriate to your level
  • Time Commitment: 1 practice + 1 game weekly (Sept-Mar)
  • Support Available: Mentorship, clinics, and resources
  • Coach Coordinator: coaching@gcba.com

Coaching Development

Growing as a coach benefits both you and your players. GCBA supports your development through certification opportunities, mentorship, and ongoing education. We encourage all coaches to pursue appropriate NCCP certification and offer support throughout the process.

Certification Pathway

  • House League Coaches: FUNdamentals + Making Ethical Decisions
  • Select Coaches: Learn to Train + Making Ethical Decisions
  • Competitive Coaches: Train to Train + Making Ethical Decisions
