Frequently Asked Questions

What equipment does my child need?

Players should wear comfortable athletic clothing, athletic shoes, and bring a water bottle. The club provides jerseys for games. Basketball shoes are recommended but not required for house league.

How are teams formed?

Teams are formed based on age, skill level, and geographical location to ensure balanced competition and convenient travel for families. We try to accommodate friend requests when possible.

What is the time commitment?

House League typically runs from November to March. Each team generally has one practice and one game per week, with each session lasting about 1-1.5 hours.

How can parents get involved?

Parents can volunteer as coaches, assistant coaches, team managers, or score/timekeepers. No experience is necessary for many roles, and training is provided.
